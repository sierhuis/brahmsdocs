Bug Reports

If you experience a problem with one of our software products, please make sure to check out the known problems before reporting the problem to us.

When you submit your bug report, an e-mail will be sent to our support department who will store the bug report in a database for tracking purposes. The bug report will be associated with your name. Periodically we will post notices regarding bugs reported by our customers in the known problems section, and tips or workarounds on the FAQ page.

Frequently Asked Questions

This page contains frequently asked questions by people interested in the products of Ejenta as well as frequently asked questions asked by users. This page will also contain some tips and tricks for using the products formulated as questions.

  1. Who or what is Ejenta?
  2. What does 'Ejenta' stand for?
  3. What does Brahms stand for?
  4. What is Brahms?

1. Who or what is Ejenta?

Ejenta is a company developing work systems solutions for the healthcare industry and the space industry using agentic technology. The company uses the agentic environment called Brahms to support the development of work systems solutions.

2. What does 'Ejenta' stand for?

The term 'Ejenta', while not a dictionary term, in a colloquial or slang context, can be understood as a playful or informal way to refer to "agents" in Bengali, likely derived from the English word "agent." It's not a formal translation you'd find in a standard dictionary.

3. What does Brahms stand for?

Brahms does not refer to the famous composer but is instead an acronym for 'Business Redesign Agent-based Holistic Modeling System'.

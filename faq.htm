1. Who or what is Agent iSolutions?

Agent iSolutions is a project team at NASA Ames Research Center developing work systems solutions using agent based technology. The project team developed a multi-agent intelligent agent environment called Brahms to support the development of work systems solutions.

2. What does the 'i' in 'Agent iSolutions' stand for?

The 'I' stands for 'intelligent' to indicate the use of intelligent agents in the development of solutions. The 'I' also stands for 'internet' and 'intranet' to indicate the future direction of the team to use the intelligent agent environment to develop real-world solutions to support people on the internet and on the intranet with their consumer to business and business to business transactions.

3. What does Brahms stand for?

Brahms does not refer to the famous composer but is instead an acronym for 'Business Redesign Agent-based Holistic Modeling System'.

4. What is Brahms?

Brahms is a set of software tools to develop and simulate multi-agent models of human and machine behavior. More...
